Zenia

Social impact

A project created at the Palo Alto Global Shapers Social Impact Hackathon that provides mental health resources for the Homeless in San Jose by setting up public spaces to receive resources. Involves the creation of an awareness campaign, selling merchandise, partnering with businesses, starting clubs, and setting up booths and sessions for easy access to low-income individuals in need of mental health resources. This project carries the overall goal of tackling mental health issues to work towards overcoming homelessness.
